How it works

Three steps.
Zero jargon.

Tipped Off handles the monitoring so you don't have to wade through council websites every week.

01

Enter your postcode

Set your postcode and choose how far out you want to monitor, from ¼ mile to 2 miles. No account needed to get started.

02

We monitor for you

Our system checks planning.data.gov.uk continuously. The moment a new application lands within your radius, we catch it.

03

Get notified straight away

You get a short, readable alert by email, push notification or text. What's being built, what it means for you, and a link to the full council page if you want to dig deeper.
